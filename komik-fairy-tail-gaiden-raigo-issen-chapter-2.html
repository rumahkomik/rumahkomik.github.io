6. apakah perbedaan part, season, cour, dan chapter dlm suatu film/komik ?​


Berikut ini penjelasan mengenai perbedaan part, season, cour, serta chapter yang biasa dijumpai pada film, buku atau komik:

Part atau bagian dalam film atau komik merujuk pada subdivisi utama yang membagi cerita menjadi bagian-bagian yang lebih kecil. Contohnya, sebuah film dengan tiga bagian yang berbeda dapat disebut sebagai "trilogi". Bagian-bagian tersebut sering kali memiliki alur cerita dan fokus yang berbeda.Season atau musim dalam konteks televisi atau anime merujuk pada serangkaian episode yang ditayangkan dalam periode waktu tertentu, biasanya dalam per sekian bulan atau bahkan satu tahun. Biasanya satu musim memiliki 12 hingga 24 episode, tergantung pada genre dan durasi episode.Cour atau kouru dalam anime adalah istilah yang digunakan untuk menggambarkan pembagian episode dalam satu musim. Istilah "cour"  awalnya digunakan menggambarkan rentang episode selama satu musim. Setiap kouru biasanya terdiri atas 10 - 14 episodeChapter adalah nama lain 'bab' yang biasa kita jmpai dalam komik atau buku. Chapter merujuk pada bagian-bagian besar cerita yang terpisah satu sama lain. Setiap chapter biasanya memiliki memiliki kisah dan alur cerita tersendiri meskipun tentu masih berhubungan dengan cerita utama.Pembahasan

Secara umum bisa dikatakan bahwa part, season, dan cour mengacu pada pembagian cerita pada media audiovisual. Adapun chapter, lebih cenderung mengacu pada pembagian cerita dalam bentuk media cetak seperti komik dan buku. Meski begitu, beberapa anime juga menggunakan istilah chapter untuk mengacu pada episode dalam serinya.

Sangkuriang

Once upon a time, in West Java, there lived a wise king who had a beautiful and smart daughter. Her name was Dayang Sumbi. She liked weaving so much. Many other kings wanted to marry her. But, Dayang Sumbi didn’t want that. So, she went to a deep forest.

One day, she was weaving cloth. Suddenly, one of her tools fell to the ground. She was very tired and lazy to take it.  Then she just shouted out loud. “Oh, I fell on my needle, but I'm too lazy to take it. Can anybody take the needle for me? I will give you a special present. If you are a woman, I'll consider you as my sister. But if you are a man, I'll marry you.”

Suddenly, a male dog named Tumang came and brought her tool. Dayang Sumbi was very surprised and regretted her words. But she had to fulfill her promise to marry Tumang. Then they lived happily in a small village.

Several months later, Dayang Sumbi gave birth to a son, and named him Sangkuriang. He was a handsome and healthy boy. Sangkuriang liked hunting very much. He often went hunting in the woods using his arrow, and Tumang was always with him.

One day, Dayang Sumbi wanted to eat a deer's heart. “Sangkuriang, will you hunt for a deer today? I want to eat a deer’s heart so much” asked Dayang Sumbi.

“Of course, mom. Whatever you want, I will give it to you.” Replied Sangkuriang. “Tumang, where are you? Come on, we have to hunt for a deer.”

Then Sangkuriang went to the woods with his arrow and his faithful dog. Sangkuriang wanted to give a deer’s heart to his mother, but he couldn't find any deer. Desperately, he took an arrow and shot Tumang. Then, he took Tumang’s heart for his mother, without knowing that Tumang was his father.

“I’m home.”

“Where is Tumang? I see you hunting with him.”

“I don’t know. Maybe he’s still outside. Here is the meat for our dinner.”

“Okay, wait here. I’ll cook this meat”

After Dayang Sumbi cooked the meat, they ate it. But, Dayang Sumbi still didn’t know if the meat that she ate was Tumang.

“Why don’t Tumang come back here?”

“I.... I don't know.”

“Don’t lie to me. I know that you know about that.”

“I’m sorry, mom. I killed him.”

“You killed him? Do you know what you have done?! Tumang was your father!” said Dayang Sumbi, full of anger. She hit Sangkuriang’s head with a wooden spoon and left a scar on his head. “Get out of here and don’t ever come here again!" yelled her. Them, Sangkuriang left her mother in sadness.

Many years passed and Sangkuriang became a strong young man. He wandered everywhere. One day he stopped at his own village without realizing it. There he met Dayang Sumbi. At the time Dayang Sumbi was given eternal beauty by God, so she stayed young forever. Both of them didn’t know each other. So they fell in love and decided to marry.

But then Dayang Sumbi recognized a scar on Sangkuriang’s head. She was very shocked. She knew that Sangkuriang was his son. It was impossible for them to marry. She already told him that she was his mother, but he didn’t believe that. Sangkuriang wished that they married soon. So Dayang Sumbi gave him a very difficult condition

“Sangkuriang, if you want to marry me. I have a condition for you. I want you to build a lake and a big boat in one night.  Can you do that?”

“That’s easy! I will do anything for you!”. Sangkuriang agreed. He took her challenge without any doubt. After the sunset, he went to the hill. He called many genies to help him. He commanded the genies to build a lake and make a big boat from the trees. In a short time, Sangkuriang and his genies almost finished their work

Dayang Sumbi realized that Sangkuriang would finish her order. She immediately asked all the women in the village to make light in the east. The genie thought that it was already dawn. It was time for them to leave and leave Sangkuriang alone. Without their help, Sangkuriang couldn’t finish building the lake and the big boat.

Sangkuriang was so angry. He failed to marry Dayang Sumbi. He kicked the unfinished boat with all of his power upsides down and it turned into a mountain called “Tangkuban Perahu”, until now. Tangkuban means flipped, and Perahu means ship.

The moral value of this story is to think before act, no matter what happens, you always have to be an honest person. Because every time you lie, you will continue to lie to cover up your lies.

26. Apa artinya *Kaminari no kokyu ichi no kata hekireki Issen*


“Kaminari no kokyu ichi no kata hekireki Issen” atau merupakan bahasa jepang dengan penulisan “雷 の コキュ 位置 の 方 霹靂 一線”. Untuk mendapatkan arti yang tepat dapat dilakukan dengan merubahnya menjadi bahasa inggris terlebih dahulu kemudian diterjemahkan kedalam Bahasa Indonesia. Dalam bahasa inggris kalimat tersebut memiliki arti “Thunder Breathing First Form: Thunderclap and Flash” atau dalam Bahasa Indonesia dapat diartikan menjadi “Bentuk Pertama Pernapasan Guntur: Petir dan Kilat”.

Pembahasan:

Sastra Jepang (日本文学 Nihon Bungaku) adalah karya sastra dalam bahasa Jepang, atau studi mengenai karya sastra tersebut dan pengarangnya. Di Indonesia sendiri Sastra Jepang memiliki bagiannya tersendiri. Negara yang terkenal dengan keindahan bunga sakuranya ini memang memiliki pesona yang mengagumkan. Tidak hanya itu saja, Jepang juga memiliki bahasa dan sastra yang menarik untuk dipelajari.

Daya tarik negara jepang sendiri lah yang membuat banyak peminat bagi para pelajar untuk mempelajari bahasa jepang. Jepang adalah salah satu negara maju di dunia. Belakangan Jepang juga merupakan negara dengan perekonomian terbesar ketiga di dunia. Artinya, belajar Sastra Jepang akan sangat bermanfaat dan membuka banyak sekali peluang kedepannya. Selain itu, Potensi yang besar untuk merintis karir masa depan di perusahaan Jepang yang ada di Indonesia. Karena, lulusan Sastra Jepang akan memiliki nilai lebih saat melamar kerja di perusahaan Jepang.

One day a violinist decided to play some music as he was walking through the woods. But this attracted a large fierce bear.

“How well you play,” he said to the man, to gain his confidence. “Could you teach me to play as well?”

“Of course,” answered the violinist, not fooled at all. “As long as you do what I tell you. Put your paws in the crack in this tree.”

The bear did as he was told and the musician was quick to jam his paws in the crack with a large stone. The bear was stuck tight.

The next time the violinist played, he attracted a lion, and the same scene was repeated. This time the beast was caught in a trap and ended up hanging by its tail from a tree. Then it was the turn of a tiger, which was captured by a similar trick.

When the three wild beasts managed to free themselves, the chased after the violinist, seeking revenge; but they found him in the company of a new friend: a huge woodsman with a giant axe, who chased the animals away. And so the clever violinist Crossed the forest in safety.
